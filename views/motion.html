Concept

Personally, I don't enjoy the taste of beer but I love the idea of it. I was inspired to create a short bumper ad, although I have to admit that it is pushing the time limit a little.
The target audience are young adults (20 and up) and adults, who have grown up with the idea of beer as a reward at the end of a hard day being repetitively shown in media. Befitting this idea, the overall mood of the video is supposed to be peaceful, almost like breathing out after holding your breath for too long.
In order to play into the nostalgia a little more, I chose a serif font.
